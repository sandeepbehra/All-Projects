Vallabh Bhai Patel

1875-1950

Iron man of India

Sardar Vallabhbhai Patel (1875–1950) was one of India’s most revered freedom fighters, statesmen, and leaders. Known as the "Iron Man of India," he played a pivotal role in uniting the nation during its transition from British rule to independence.Post-independence, Patel served as India’s first Deputy Prime Minister and Home Minister. He is most celebrated for his monumental task of integrating over 560 princely states into the Indian Union, a feat that required diplomatic skill, firmness, and vision. Patel's efforts ensured that India emerged as a united and sovereign nation.Sardar Patel was instrumental in establishing the All India Services, envisioning a robust administrative framework to uphold national unity and governance.
